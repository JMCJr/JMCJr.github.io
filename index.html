What The Duck?

DUCK

/d…ôk/

noun: duck; plural noun: ducks; plural noun: duck; noun: duck boat; plural noun: duck boats; noun: duckboat; plural noun: duckboats

  • a waterbird with a broad blunt bill, short legs, webbed feet, and a waddling gait.
  • a pure white thin-shelled bivalve mollusk found off the Atlantic coasts of America.
  • an amphibious transport vehicle.
  • a delicious protein.
